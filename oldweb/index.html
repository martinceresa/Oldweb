Welcome to my research Web Page

PhD student in Computer Science at CIFASIS. Advised by Mauro Jaskelioff.

Before coming to CIFASIS, I was a student in the deparment of Computer Science at Universidad Nacional de Rosario from where I gratuated in March, 2015. In 2013, I worked as intern at IMDEA(Madrid, Spain) under the tutelage of Benedikt Smichdt, in the development of autmatic proofs in EasyCrypt.

My interests include: theoretical computer science, programming languages theory, type theory, category theory, mathematics, and logic. I am currently working in Parallel Programming, more specific trying to find a good model.

In my recreative moments, I like to play football, grab some beers with some friends in Antares (a local pub) and read, read and read some more!
